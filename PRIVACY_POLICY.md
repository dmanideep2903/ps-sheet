# Privacy Policy for PS Sheet

**Last Updated:** December 3, 2025

## Introduction

PS Sheet ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our attendance and work log management application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
- **Employee Data:** Name, email address, employee ID, and role
- **Biometric Data:** Face recognition data captured during attendance check-in/check-out (stored locally only)
- **Work Records:** Attendance timestamps, work log entries, task assignments, and related metadata
- **Network Information:** IP address and device MAC address for attendance verification

### 2. Usage Data
- Login/logout timestamps
- Punch-in/punch-out records
- Work log submissions and approvals
- Administrative actions performed within the app

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:
- **Attendance Tracking:** Recording employee check-in/check-out times
- **Work Management:** Managing task assignments, submissions, and approvals
- **Authentication:** Verifying user identity through face recognition
- **Reporting:** Generating attendance and productivity reports
- **Security:** Preventing unauthorized access and ensuring data integrity

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Face recognition models and captured images are stored **locally on your device only**
- Biometric data is **never transmitted** to external servers
- All biometric data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption

### Server Storage
- Employee records, attendance data, and work logs are stored on your organization's configured backend server
- Data transmission is secured using HTTPS encryption
- Access is restricted to authenticated users only

## Data Sharing

We **do not sell, trade, or rent** your personal information to third parties. Your data is:
- Only accessible to authorized administrators within your organization
- Not shared with external parties except as required by law
- Not used for marketing or advertising purposes

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data stored in the system
- **Request correction** of inaccurate information
- **Request deletion** of your account and associated data (subject to organizational retention policies)
- **Opt-out** of face recognition (alternative authentication methods may be provided by your organization)

## Data Retention

- **Biometric Data:** Retained locally until you uninstall the app or delete your profile
- **Attendance Records:** Retained according to your organization's policies (typically for legal/compliance purposes)
- **Work Logs:** Retained for the duration of employment and as required by organizational policies

## Children's Privacy

PS Sheet is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Third-Party Services

The app may connect to:
- **Backend Server:** Self-hosted or cloud-hosted API server (configured by your organization)
- **Network Services:** For time synchronization and connectivity checks

No third-party analytics or tracking services are integrated into the app.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app and on our distribution channels. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Compliance

PS Sheet is designed to comply with:
- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation) for users in the EU
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act) for California residents
- **Local data protection laws** as applicable
